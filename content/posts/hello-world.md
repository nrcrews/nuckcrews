I've been meaning to start writing for a while. The usual excuse
(*nothing's quite ready to share yet*) finally got old, so here we are.

## What this is

A place to think out loud in public. Mostly:

- **Technology** I'm using, or breaking
- **AI**, with all the caveats that implies in 2026
- **Things I'm building**, in various states of finish
- **Opinions** I'm still working out

If you came here looking for thought leadership, you're going to leave
disappointed. The bar is "interesting to me, and hopefully one other
person."

## How it'll work

Plain markdown, posts in this repo, deployed on Vercel. No comments,
no newsletter. Yet. If something resonates, find me elsewhere.

More soon.
